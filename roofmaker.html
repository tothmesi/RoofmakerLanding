Features

Roofmaker helps architects to design, build and audit roof constructions by collecting geometrical details and main rules of roof covering in an easily accessible way.


Roof covering

Informations about roof tiles and roof sheets including geometry and required roof pitch. Collection of the main rules of roofing.

Thermal analysis

Calculate the main thermical parameters of the most commonly used layers and construction detailes using drag 'n drop

Building diagnostics

Collection of common problems with roof constructions and checklists for inspecting the reasons of flooding
